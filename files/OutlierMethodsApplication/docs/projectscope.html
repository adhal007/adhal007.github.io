Overview

The outlier sum statistic is a statistical method to identify differential features between 2 sets of data based on differences in the outliers in the 2 cohorts. Mostly, this sort of difference in outliers is observed in complex biological diseases (for example cancers) - where outlier individuals for genes potentially associated with the disease will be found.
